Our Story

Dedicated to preserving and sharing Cameroon's rich cultural heritage through innovative AI technology.

The Mission Behind CULTIA

We are a team of software engineering students from Cameroon, driven by a passion to bridge the gap between technology and tradition. We noticed that the digital age was pulling younger generations away from their roots.

Our vision was simple: Use the very technology that captures attention to bring cultural heritage back into focus. CULTIA is our friendly, interactive solution to preserve the beautiful diversity of Cameroon's 250+ ethnic groups.

Our Mission

To democratize access to cultural knowledge by creating an intelligent, respectful platform that preserves and celebrates human diversity.

Our Vision

We envision a world where cultural boundaries become bridges, and technology serves as the guardian of our collective human heritage.

  • Globally connected communities
  • Universal access to education
  • Sustainable preservation practices
