# Quick Suggestions Update

## Changes Made

1. **Updated Quick Suggestion Buttons** - Replaced the generic cultural questions with Cameroon tribe specific questions:
   - "Bamileke Tribe History & Culture" - "Tell me about the history and culture of the Bamileke tribe"
   - "Bassa People Religious Beliefs" - "What are the religious beliefs and traditions of the Bassa people?"
   - "Fulani Tribe Marriage Customs" - "Describe the marriage customs and social structure of the Fulani tribe"

2. **Updated Welcome Message** - Added a note about the quick suggestion buttons:
   - Added: "Click the quick suggestion buttons below to get started."

3. **Updated Input Placeholder** - Made it more specific to Cameroon:
   - Changed from: "Ask me about cultures, traditions, or travel advice..."
   - Changed to: "Ask me about Cameroonian tribes, their cultures, traditions, or history..."

## Functionality
- When users click any of the quick suggestion buttons, the corresponding question will automatically appear in the search bar
- Users can then press Enter or click the send button to submit the question
- The buttons are styled consistently with the existing design
- The functionality is the same as before, just with Cameroon-focused content

## Benefits
- More relevant to the Cameroonian tribal culture focus of the application
- Provides quick access to common questions about major tribes
- Maintains the same user experience with improved content# Quick Suggestions Update
